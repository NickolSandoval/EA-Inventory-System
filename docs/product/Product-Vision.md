# EA Inventory System

## Product Vision Document

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Approved
**Last Updated:** June 2026

---

# 1. Product Overview

EA Inventory System is a configurable web platform designed to help small service-based businesses manage inventory, assets, operational activities and business growth through a centralized and scalable solution.

The product is initially being developed to support an audio services company for events, but it is designed from the beginning to be adaptable to multiple business types that rely on physical assets and equipment.

---

# 2. Problem Statement

Many small businesses manage their inventory and assets through spreadsheets, messaging applications, paper records or informal processes.

As businesses grow, these methods often lead to:

* Loss of equipment and assets.
* Lack of visibility regarding equipment availability.
* Difficulty tracking asset movements.
* Limited operational control.
* Inconsistent maintenance records.
* Challenges when managing multiple locations.

These issues reduce efficiency, increase operational risks and make business growth more difficult.

---

# 3. Proposed Solution

EA Inventory System will provide a centralized platform that allows businesses to register, organize, track and monitor their assets and operational activities.

The platform will be configurable without requiring code modifications, enabling different businesses to adapt the system to their own operational needs.

The solution will focus on simplicity, scalability and operational visibility.

---

# 4. Target Users

## Business Owner

Responsible for monitoring operations, reviewing business performance and making strategic decisions.

## Administrator

Responsible for configuring the platform and managing business data.

## Operator

Responsible for registering and tracking inventory movements and operational activities.

## Technician

Responsible for maintenance activities and equipment status updates.

---

# 5. Product Objectives

## Short-Term Objectives

* Build a functional MVP.
* Validate real operational processes.
* Improve inventory visibility and control.
* Gather feedback from actual users.

## Mid-Term Objectives

* Create a reusable solution for different service-based businesses.
* Improve operational efficiency.
* Standardize inventory management processes.

## Long-Term Objectives

* Evolve into a multi-company SaaS platform.
* Support multiple branches and organizations.
* Become a scalable business management solution for small and medium-sized service companies.

---

# 6. Initial Scope

The first version of the product will focus on providing foundational inventory and asset management capabilities, including:

* Business configuration.
* Branch management.
* Asset categorization.
* Inventory management.
* Basic operational dashboards.

Detailed functionality will be defined within the MVP Scope Document.

---

# 7. Product Principles

The following principles will guide all product decisions:

* Simplicity before complexity.
* Configuration before customization through code.
* Scalability from the beginning.
* Business value before technical complexity.
* Incremental development through continuous feedback.
* Reusability across multiple business types.

---

# 8. Future Vision

The product roadmap is structured around three stages:

## Stage 1 — Business Validation

Validate the platform within a real service-based business environment.

## Stage 2 — Configurable Product

Expand the platform into a reusable solution that can support multiple types of businesses without code changes.

## Stage 3 — SaaS Platform

Transform the solution into a multi-tenant SaaS platform capable of serving multiple organizations and branches from a single system.

---

# 9. Success Metrics

The success of the product will be measured through:

* Reduction in inventory tracking errors.
* Increased visibility of asset availability.
* Improved operational efficiency.
* Adoption by business users.
* Reusability across different organizations.
* Ability to scale without major architectural changes.

---

# 10. Vision Statement

To provide small service-based businesses with a simple, configurable and scalable platform that enables effective inventory and asset management, supports operational growth and serves as a foundation for future digital transformation.
